About Me

I'm Emmanuel Olatunji, a passionate full-stack developer born and raised in the bustling city of Lagos, Nigeria. With over 5 years of experience in software development, I've dedicated my career to creating technology solutions that address real African challenges.

My journey began at the University of Lagos where I studied Computer Science, and since then, I've worked with startups across Nigeria, Ghana, and Kenya, building everything from fintech platforms to educational technology solutions for underserved communities.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me mentoring young developers through local tech hubs, volunteering at community centers teaching digital literacy, or exploring the vibrant markets of Lagos for inspiration.
